What is Jackbox Games? +

Jackbox Games is the studio behind popular party games for popular games like UNKNOWN JACK, Quiplash, Fibbage, Drawful, Trivia Murder Party and more! Think of it as the child of traditional board games like “charades”… the kid who dropped out of college and later earned the respect of his parents by starting a music club.

You can learn about us through articles, publications, exchanges or social media. Maybe you heard about us from a friend. We are happy to meet you in any situation: we are here to make parties and meetings with fans unique, fun and unforgettable.

What is a Party Pack? +

We create a variety of games designed to entertain everyone in the room via Zoom calls or Twitch streams. Designing games, writing games, mini-games, stealth games... no partygoer is left behind.

Every year since 2014 we release a new collection of games packaged as the Jackbox Party Pack. In each package you will find 5 different games that can accommodate 1-8 players. If you have a lot of people in your group, then (1) we're happy to be popular and (2) most of our games get the crowd playing, meaning they can keep jumping and having fun!

We also have special horror games like Drawful 2 that are available outside of party packs.

I purchased the party package! Do my friends or family need to do anything to play? +

All your players need is a phone or internet-enabled device! Take a look with us and you're sure to enjoy long-lasting eye contact: no apps or additional purchases required to play. They only need a mobile device; Internet access; and to see the game screen.

How do I get a Jackbox Game Started? +

You purchased the Party Pack. You've gathered your team. All you need is to start the game and play! Here we have helpful video tutorials and step-by-step instructions on how to get all your players hooked into the game. This page also includes information on how to access additional channels and options for each game.

The important thing is to ensure that everyone in your party (in person or remotely) can see and hear the game screen. This allows them to access room codes, work together, see what's going on in the game, and play together.

What should I do if I have problems playing the Jackbox game? +

We have a great website here with lots of tips and tricks. You can also donate tickets to our support team. They try to respond within 1-3 business hours. Holidays may last a little longer if we are affected.

However, our strong recommendation is that if a player's mobile device disconnects during gameplay... they should try updating their browser. Most of the time they meet right away. If not, please let us know. As we say: no partygoer is left behind.
